Best Ranking Movies According to Yen

My top 3 movies of all-time.


Amelie

I relate to the main character who is introverted, very imaginative, and often want to help others yet also need courage to take her own risks in life.

Howl's Moving Castle

Love the fantasy world as well as the depiction of youth depends on one's feelings of love for another as well as courage to do what is difficult.

Life of Pi

Philosophical, a story about struggling to survive through seemingly insurmountable odds.
